Our Founding

In 2002, UpNorth Travel was founded by John Smith with a passion for exploring the beauty and diversity of the world. Initially, the agency focused on providing personalized travel solutions to individuals and small groups seeking unique and unforgettable travel experiences.

The Growth

Through hard work and dedication to customer satisfaction, UpNorth Travel quickly established a reputation as a trusted and reliable partner in the travel industry. Over the years, the agency expanded its services to include flight bookings, hotel reservations, car rentals, tour packages, and travel insurance, among others. Today, UpNorth Travel is known for its attention to detail, sustainable tourism practices, and its ability to create customized travel itineraries that cater to the unique interests and preferences of its clients.

Our Bussines Model

UpNorth Travel's business model is centered around providing personalized and sustainable travel solutions to individuals and groups seeking to explore the beauty and diversity of the northern regions of the world. The agency generates revenue by offering a range of travel services, including flight bookings, hotel reservations, car rentals, tour packages, and travel insurance, among others. UpNorth Travel's competitive advantage lies in its ability to create customized travel itineraries that cater to the unique interests and preferences of its clients, while also promoting responsible and sustainable tourism practices. The agency's focus on customer satisfaction, attention to detail, and commitment to sustainable tourism has helped to establish its reputation as a trusted and reliable partner in the travel industry.
